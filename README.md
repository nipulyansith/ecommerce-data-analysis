## Results Summary

The analysis revealed several important insights:

- A small number of countries contribute significantly higher revenue compared to others.
- There is a strong positive relationship between product quantity and total sales value.
- Sales patterns fluctuate over time, indicating possible seasonal effects or promotional impacts.
- Regression analysis shows that purchase quantity is a significant predictor of revenue.

These findings demonstrate how data analytics can support business decision-making, including marketing strategy, pricing optimization, and inventory planning.
